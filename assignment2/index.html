About us

Welcome to Sweet Life, where happiness and sweetness intertwine to create an unforgettable ice cream experience. As a beloved neighborhood ice cream shop located at 16 Stirling, Derrimut, VIC 3026, we take pride in our rich history and commitment to delivering delectable frozen treats. Our menu boasts a tempting selection of flavors, including classic Vanilla, fruity Strawberry, and indulgent Chocolate. With every scoop crafted using the finest ingredients, we invite you to join us on a journey of taste and indulgence at Sweet Life.

Our new product

Introducing our new line of ice creams! We've got something for everyone, starting with our Balls Lemon and Berry ice-cream in a bowl. This refreshing flavor is perfect for those hot summer days. If you're a chocolate lover, try our Balls Chocolate ice-cream in a bowl - it's rich, creamy, and oh-so-decadent. Finally, for a truly unique and indulgent experience, try our Balls Valani ice-cream in a bowl. Made with real Valani nuts, this flavor is nutty, buttery, and simply divine. All of our ice creams are made with the highest-quality ingredients and are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. So why wait? Head to our store and try all three today!
